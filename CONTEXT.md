# Personal Site

The public English-language portfolio and writing site through which Marco presents his work, tools, and Articles.

## Language

**Article**:
A published piece of English-language writing with one identity and public URL.
_Avoid_: Post

**Cover Image**:
Optional editorial media displayed as part of an Article. Its alternative text is mandatory whenever the image is present.

**Social Image**:
Optional preview media used when an Article is shared externally. An Article without one uses the site-wide Social Image.
_Avoid_: Cover Image

**Uses**:
The curated account of hardware, software, services, and tools Marco currently uses.
_Avoid_: Stack

**AI Tools**:
The curated account of AI agents and supporting tools Marco uses or recommends.
_Avoid_: AI, Agents
